About HomieStay

HomieStay is a lightweight booking system designed for homestay properties. Our system makes it easy for guests to view property information and make reservations directly through WhatsApp.

Features

  • Simple Browsing: View property images, prices, descriptions, and availability
  • Easy Booking: Book directly through WhatsApp with just a few clicks
  • QR Code Access: Scan to instantly access property information
  • User-Friendly: Clean, intuitive interface for both hosts and guests

How It Works

  1. Browse available properties and their details
  2. Select your preferred dates and room options
  3. Click the "Book Now" button to generate a pre-filled WhatsApp message
  4. Send the message to confirm your booking with the property owner
